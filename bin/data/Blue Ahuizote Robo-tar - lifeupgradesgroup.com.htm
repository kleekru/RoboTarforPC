The Ahuizote is a mythical Latin American beast with a single claw on his tail...

We used this image to illustrate the new and exciting one-handed opportunity for guitar players.  Whether you've never played guitar, or, you've played for years, the Blue Ahuizote Robo-tar is an exciting and innovative new way to play nearly any song.

This incredible new device attaches easily and temporarily to the neck of a standard guitar.  The player chooses the chords for the song they want to play and then uses a foot to activate each chord at their own tempo while strumming along with one hand.  It won't matter if you are a left or right handed strummer.

Anyone can learn to play and sing their favorite guitar tunes in one day.

The product is currently being redesigned with a sleek new look and robust quality.  Our goal is to provide you with options that match your style and make a lasting impression on  your friends, peers and fans.

Life Upgrades supports United States Disabled American Veterans and the American Stroke and American Heart Associations.  We are looking for opportunities to make this device available for those that have lost the use of one of their arms or hands in service to our country or due to stroke.  Please contact us using the form below!  We want to know you are interested!  This product was developed with you in mind!
